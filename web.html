Two ways to partner

One team behind you, whichever path you take.

Whether you broker loans or fund them yourself, Rocket Pro meets you where you are — and helps you get licensed for it.

Wholesale · Broker

Originate. We underwrite and fund.

You bring the borrower and submit the loan; Rocket Pro underwrites and funds it. Lighter capital requirements — a broker license plus a surety bond in each state you work.

  • Mortgage Broker License (by state)
  • Surety bond, often $20k+ to start
  • Lower barrier — the fastest path to independent
Correspondent · Lender

Fund in your name. Then sell to us.

You underwrite and fund loans on a warehouse line, then sell them to Rocket Pro or other investors. More control and upside — with higher net worth, audited financials, and a warehouse line.

  • Mortgage Lender / Correspondent License
  • Warehouse line + audited financials
  • More independence and margin per loan
